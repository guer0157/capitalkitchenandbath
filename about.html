About Us

Your Trusted Kitchen & Bathroom Renovations in Ottawa

At Capital Kitchen & Bath®, we specialize in high-quality kitchen and bathroom renovations in Ottawa, built around craftsmanship, clear communication, and a professional renovation process from start to finish. Our founders bring over 25 years of combined experience in the Ottawa renovation industry, working on everything from full bathroom remodels to complex kitchen renovations in both houses and condominiums. That experience has shaped how we work today: with structure, transparency, and a relentless focus on doing things right the first time.

Built on Experience, Focused on Quality

Renovating kitchens and bathrooms is not general construction — it requires precision, planning, and coordination between multiple licensed trades. That’s why in 2026 we’ve chosen to focus exclusively on kitchens and bathrooms, rather than trying to do everything.

By specializing, we’re able to:
  • Anticipate common issues before walls are opened
  • Build realistic timelines and budgets
  • Coordinate plumbing, electrical, tiling, and inspections properly
  • Deliver consistent, long-lasting results

We don’t cut corners, rush jobs, or rely on “good enough.” Our work is designed to last — both structurally and visually.

Clear Communication, No Surprises

One of the biggest frustrations homeowners face during a renovation is poor communication. We’ve built our process specifically to avoid that.

From the first consultation, we focus on:
  • Clearly defining the scope of work
  • Setting expectations around timelines and budgets
  • Explaining what’s included — and what isn’t
  • Addressing risks upfront, not after the fact

You’ll always know where your project stands, what decisions are needed, and how changes are handled. No guesswork. No last-minute surprises.

A Professional Process

We believe that a successful renovation starts before construction begins. That’s why we take the time to properly assess each project and client fit.

Our process includes:
  • Budget alignment before quoting
  • Trade and permit planning (especially for condos)
  • Written scopes of work
  • Structured change-order management when scope changes

This approach isn’t for everyone — and that’s intentional.

Proudly Serving Ottawa

We proudly serve homeowners across Ottawa and surrounding areas, delivering kitchen and bathroom renovations that are well-planned, professionally managed, and built to last. If you’re considering a kitchen or bathroom renovation and want a contractor who prioritizes quality, communication, and accountability, we’d be happy to talk.
