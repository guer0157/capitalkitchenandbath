Our Kitchen Renovation Process

A successful kitchen renovation starts long before demolition day.

  • 1. Initial Consultation & Fit Check

    We begin by understanding:

    • How you use your kitchen today
    • Desired layout and functionality
    • Budget alignment
    • Timeline constraints

    This ensures the project is realistic and a good fit before moving forward.

  • 2. Scope Definition & Pre-Construction Planning

    Before work begins, we clearly define:

    • Cabinet and layout assumptions
    • Appliance and fixture requirements
    • Electrical and plumbing scope
    • Permit and inspection needs

    Clear documentation at this stage prevents delays and misunderstandings later.

  • 3. Construction & Trade Coordination

    We manage all aspects of your kitchen renovation, including:

    • Demolition and disposal
    • Framing and carpentry
    • Plumbing and electrical
    • Cabinet coordination and installation
    • Flooring, backsplash, and finishes
    • Inspections and final walkthrough

    All work is completed using licensed, experienced trades.

  • 4. Change Management (When Required)

    If scope changes or unforeseen conditions arise:

    • Changes are documented in writing
    • Costs and schedule impacts are explained upfront
    • Work proceeds only after approval

    This protects both the client and the project.
